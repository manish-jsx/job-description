Job Title: Content Writer (Full-time/Part-time)

Job Description:

We are seeking a talented and creative Content Writer to join our team and support our growing freelancing business. The ideal candidate will have a passion for writing, a knack for storytelling, and the ability to produce high-quality content that engages and informs our audience. This role is flexible, offering both full-time and part-time opportunities.

Responsibilities:

  • Content Creation: Write, edit, and proofread content for various platforms including blogs, websites, social media, newsletters, and marketing materials.
  • Research: Conduct thorough research on industry-related topics to produce original and insightful content.
  • SEO Optimization: Implement SEO best practices to ensure content is optimized for search engines and can drive organic traffic.
  • Content Strategy: Collaborate on content strategies and editorial calendars to plan and execute content campaigns.
  • Feedback and Revisions: Incorporate feedback from clients and team members to improve and refine content.
  • Brand Voice: Maintain a consistent brand voice and style across all content.
  • Project Management: Manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines consistently.

Qualifications:

  • Experience: Proven experience as a content writer, copywriter, or similar role. Portfolio of published articles.
  • Skills: Excellent writing and editing skills in English. Strong research skills and attention to detail.
  • SEO Knowledge: Familiarity with SEO best practices and content management systems (CMS).
  • Creativity: Ability to generate creative ideas and translate them into compelling content.
  • Adaptability: Ability to adapt writing style to different audiences and formats.
  • Communication: Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to communicate effectively with team members and clients.
  • Education: Degree in English, Journalism, Marketing, or a related field is preferred but not mandatory.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Technical Skills: Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and content management systems (like WordPress).
  • Marketing Experience: Understanding of digital marketing principles and strategies.
  • Social Media: Experience in managing social media accounts and creating engaging social media content.

Working Conditions:

  • Location: Remote, with occasional in-person meetings if necessary.
  • Hours: Flexible hours with the ability to meet deadlines. Both full-time and part-time positions are available.
  • Compensation: Competitive salary based on experience and qualifications.
